About Me

I am currently a Pre-Business student at Brigham Young University and I plan on applying to the Information Systems program ummer of 2022. I have always had a passion for problem-solving and creating new ways to overcome obstacles. Throughout my education, this love for problem-solving has transferred to my love for coding and learning new programming languages. Developing skills such as critical thinking has been fulfilling in my education thus far and I look forward to continuing to expand my abilities.


For two years I had the opportunity to serve a full-time proselyting mission for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints in the greater St. Louis, MO area. This experience changed my life and taught me the importance of charity and kindness. My service not only helped others overcome struggles in their personal lives, but it also helped me change the way I view and treat other people.



One of my favorite hobbies is playing the bagpipes. Beginning with my grandma immigrating to the United States from Scotland and starting a family of her own in Utah, I am proud of my Scottish heritage. Starting at 11 years old, I learned how to play the bagpipes from my dad. We are both currently members of the Salt Lake Scots Pipe Band, serving others in the community with our musical abilities. I have entered multiple competitions in Utah, both solo and with the band, and I love continuing to grow my talents.
